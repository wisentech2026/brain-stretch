隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 6 月

「换换脑子」（以下简称"本服务"）由 Wisentech 运营。我们重视你的隐私。本政策说明我们如何处理你的信息。简而言之：我们几乎不收集任何个人数据。

我们收集什么

本服务的设计原则是数据最小化。具体而言：

  • 训练记录：你的练习成绩和趋势数据仅存储在你自己设备的浏览器本地存储（localStorage）中，不会上传到我们的服务器。清除浏览器数据即会删除这些记录。
  • 不收集账户信息：本服务无需注册或登录，我们不收集你的姓名、邮箱或电话。
  • 匿名访问统计：我们可能使用注重隐私的分析工具（如 Plausible）来了解整体访问量。这类工具不使用 Cookie，不追踪个人身份。

广告

本服务可能展示由 Google AdSense 提供的广告。Google 作为第三方供应商，可能使用 Cookie 根据你过往的访问投放广告。你可以通过访问 Google 广告设置 来管理个性化广告偏好。

第三方服务

当你选择通过 Buy Me a Coffee 支持我们时，相关支付由该第三方平台处理，受其各自的隐私政策约束。我们不接触你的支付卡信息。

儿童隐私

本服务不针对 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集其个人信息。

你的权利

由于我们几乎不在服务器端存储你的个人数据，你对本地数据拥有完全控制权——随时可通过清除浏览器数据删除全部记录。

联系我们

如对本政策有任何疑问，请联系：hello@wisentech.com

Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Brain Stretch ("the Service") is operated by Wisentech. We respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information. In short: we collect almost no personal data.

What we collect

  • Training records: Your scores and progress are stored only in your own browser's local storage and are never uploaded to our servers. Clearing your browser data deletes them.
  • No account data: The Service requires no sign-up or login. We do not collect your name, email, or phone number.
  • Anonymous analytics: We may use privacy-friendly analytics (e.g. Plausible) to understand overall traffic. Such tools use no cookies and do not track individual identity.

Advertising

The Service may display ads served by Google AdSense. As a third-party vendor, Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can manage personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

Third-party services

If you choose to support us via Buy Me a Coffee, payment is processed by that third-party platform under its own privacy policy. We never access your payment card details.

Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

Your rights

Because we store almost no personal data on our servers, you have full control over your local data and may delete all records at any time by clearing your browser data.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact hello@wisentech.com.
